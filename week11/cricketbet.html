FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How does the seat bidding process work?

Our seat bidding system allows you to bid on premium seats for major cricket matches. You can place bids on available seats, and the highest bidder when the auction closes wins the seats. All bids are binding, so ensure you're committed before placing a bid.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), UPI payments (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and digital wallets. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.

Can I cancel or modify my bid?

Bids cannot be canceled once placed, as they are legally binding commitments. However, you may increase your bid amount at any time before the auction closes by placing a new higher bid.

How are betting odds calculated?

Our odds are calculated by a team of expert analysts considering team form, player performance, pitch conditions, weather, and other relevant factors. Odds may fluctuate based on these variables and betting patterns.

What happens if a match is canceled?

If a match is canceled before a ball is bowled, all seat bids will be refunded in full, and any placed bets will be voided with stakes returned. If the match starts but is later abandoned, our cancellation policy will apply based on the specific circumstances.
